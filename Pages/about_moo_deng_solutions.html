A Passionate Team

Here at Moo-Deng, we are passionate about bringing our customer's ideas to life. We pride ourselves in hardwork, punctuality and dedication. We are currently a 2-man team, with more position opening coming soon.

Our Goals and Missions

Customer Satisfaction

Diligence is our core value. We work hard on every project and do our very best to satisfy our clients. Punctuality permiates every meeting and decision at Moo-Deng. If all fails, we will always be honest and upfront with our clients.

A Leader in Africa's Webdev's Industry

We thrive to become a leader in Africa's web development industry. Our goal is to expand our businesses to neighboring countries.

Youth Empowerment

Empowering the youth of Rwanda, and more broadly Africa, is one of our long-term goals. Through our hiring practices, we hope to help the youth achieve their long-term career goals and get valuable experience in the Tech/ Web development industry.

Inclusivity

In almost every society, disabled people and minorities often fall into poverty and have limited ways of getting out. We aim to address this problem through hiring more people with diverse backgrounds and/or disabilities.
